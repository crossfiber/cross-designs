The Operator

Hi, I'm Aiden.

I run Cross Designs, a one-person web studio. I've been building sites for a few years: small businesses, independent creators, and brands that need a real online presence without disappearing into another Squarespace template.

Most of what I do falls into three buckets: redesigning sites that look like every other site in their category, building from scratch when there's no site at all, and tightening on-page SEO so the work gets found. Every build ships as hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JS. No bloat, no page builders, no surprises in the lighthouse score.

How a build
actually goes.

~ four steps ~
01

Brief + Diagnosis

Free call. We talk through the business, the audience, what's currently working, and what isn't. Most projects come back with a one-page diagnosis before any design starts.

20-30 min
02

Design Direction

Visual identity, type, color, signature elements. You see a real direction (not a Pinterest board) and sign off before any code gets written.

1 week
03

Hand-Coded Build

Front-end in HTML, CSS, and JS, no template starter. Mobile and desktop are designed separately. SEO and schema are baked in from the first commit.

2-3 weeks
04

Ship + Handoff

Deployed to your hosting (or mine) with a written handoff: how to update content, where the assets live, what to do post-launch.
